# Sulla Desktop Documentation

Welcome to the official documentation for **Sulla Desktop** -- your AI-powered executive assistant for the desktop.

Sulla Desktop is an Electron-based application that brings AI assistant capabilities directly to your computer. It combines powerful language models with workflow automation to help you accomplish tasks more efficiently, whether you are managing daily routines, automating repetitive processes, or leveraging AI for research and analysis.

## What Sulla Desktop Does

- **AI Assistant**: Chat with state-of-the-art language models (remote or local) to get help with tasks, research, writing, and decision-making.
- **Workflow Automation**: Build and run automated workflows using n8n integration, from daily news briefs to complex multi-step business processes.
- **External Service Integration**: Connect your favorite tools and services (email, Slack, and more) so your AI assistant can act on your behalf.
- **Cross-Platform**: Available on macOS, Windows, and Linux.
